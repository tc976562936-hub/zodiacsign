Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2023

No Data Collection • 100% Offline • Complete Privacy

Thank you for choosing Stellar Zodiac. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to your privacy and how we handle information in our application.

Privacy-First Design

Stellar Zodiac is built with privacy as a fundamental principle. The application operates entirely on your device, requires no internet connection, and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

1. Information Collection

Stellar Zodiac does not collect any personal information. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No location data or device identifiers
  • No usage analytics or crash reports
  • No payment information (the app is free with no purchases)
  • No access to your photos, contacts, or other personal data

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores two types of data locally on your device:

  1. App Preferences: Settings like theme preference or display options
  2. Saved Zodiac Signs: Your bookmarked/favorite zodiac signs for quick access

All this data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

3. No Third-Party Services

Stellar Zodiac does not integrate with any third-party services that might collect data, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics services
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services

4. Offline Operation

The app is designed to function completely offline. All zodiac information, images, and functionality are included within the app package. No internet connection is required for any feature, ensuring that no data can be transmitted from your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Stellar Zodiac does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
