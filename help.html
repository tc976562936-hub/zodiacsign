Help & FAQ

Get the most out of Stellar Zodiac with our comprehensive guide and frequently asked questions.

Getting Started

Welcome to Stellar Zodiac! This app is designed to be intuitive and easy to use. Here's how to get started:

1

Explore Zodiac Signs

Open the app and browse through all 12 zodiac signs. Tap on any sign to view detailed information including dates, traits, elements, and compatibility.

2

Save Your Favorites

Tap the bookmark icon on any zodiac sign to save it to your favorites. This creates a quick-access list for signs you reference most often.

3

Create & Share Cards

Generate beautiful zodiac cards with information about any sign. Share them with friends or save to your device's photo library.

Tip: Offline Access

Remember, Stellar Zodiac works completely offline! All content is included in the app, so you can access zodiac information anytime, anywhere without an internet connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require an internet connection?

No! Stellar Zodiac is designed to work completely offline. All zodiac information, images, and functionality are included in the app download. No internet connection is required for any feature.

Does the app collect any of my personal data?

Absolutely not. Stellar Zodiac does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Any data you create in the app (like favorite zodiac signs) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

How do I save a zodiac sign to my favorites?

When viewing any zodiac sign, look for the bookmark icon (usually in the top right corner). Tap this icon to add or remove the sign from your favorites. You can access all your favorite signs from the "Favorites" tab in the app.

Can I share zodiac information with friends?

Yes! Stellar Zodiac allows you to create beautiful, customizable zodiac cards. After creating a card, you can share it via messaging apps, email, or social media. You can also save the card directly to your photo library.

How do I change the app's theme or appearance?

Go to the Settings section of the app (look for the gear icon). There you can choose between light and dark themes, adjust text size, and customize other display preferences.

Is the app available for Android or other platforms?

Currently, Stellar Zodiac is only available for iOS devices (iPhone, iPad, iPod Touch). We may consider expanding to other platforms in the future based on user demand.

How do I delete my favorite zodiac signs?

You can remove signs from your favorites by tapping the bookmark icon again when viewing that sign. To clear all favorites at once, go to Settings and look for the "Clear Favorites" option.

Troubleshooting

App crashes on launch

If the app crashes immediately after opening, try these steps:

  1. Force close the app and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Ensure your iOS is updated to the latest version
  4. Delete and reinstall the app (your favorites will be lost unless backed up)

Can't save zodiac cards to photos

If you're having trouble saving cards to your photo library:

  1. Check that Stellar Zodiac has permission to access your Photos
  2. Go to Settings > Privacy > Photos and ensure Stellar Zodiac is enabled
  3. Make sure you have enough storage space on your device
